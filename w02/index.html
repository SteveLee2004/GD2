Emma Burke

Interviewed by Steve Lee

What music do you like to listen to while working?
- Anything without words; instrumental or classical music, and also soundtrack. Interstellar is the most recent soundtrack I listened to.

Name three designers whose work you find inspiring.

  • Ordinary Folk
  • Kyle Cooper
I find them interesting because I am very interested in motion graphics and title sequence designs.

Name something (a format, an item) you want to learn how to design but haven't had the chance to try to yet.
- Title sequence and packaging

If you were a font, you'd be (you can't say helvetica or comic sans-- both jokes are played). Explain why?
- Avant Garde Gothic, because it has a personality but not too ornamented.

Name one website that you rely on (try to avoid a platform like google/amazon/pinterest/etc).
- Behance

Is there a movie which you wish you could remake a title sequence of?
- Coraline. I would make it more subtle and vague in a way it starts out happy but hints that something is really wrong.
Just like the title sequence of IT: Welcome to Derry.
